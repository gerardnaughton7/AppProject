Welcome to JustDoIt

The app which will help you organise your day to day tasks.

With simple and minimalist design it is super easy to use and navigate

Here is just a step by step guide to the app:
- 3 navigation tabs About, Add and tasks
- Just tap the symbol above it navigate to next tab
- To add task simply tap on the righting "Enter a task here and press the button "Add"
- To view your tasks click on tasks symbol at the top of the screen
-Your list of tasks will be displayed
-If you wish to search for a task tap the word search and type in the task you are looking for
-Too delete a task when its completed
-Simply just swipe left on the task and a delete button should appear
-Then simply tap delete to remove task

I hope this app helps you achieve all your tasks and makes life alot simpler.
